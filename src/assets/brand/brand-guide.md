# Postscript Brand Guide (simplified)

## Colors

**Primary purple:** #5724E9

**Accent greens:** #23D1AB, #2DE34C

**Tint green:** #96F1A6 (for lighter accents)

**Dark accent:** #2C1275

## Design Language

**Shape language:** Soft pill forms and rounded corners

**Style:** Flat, vector-friendly, simple geometry, not over-illustrated

## Mascot

**Mascot = Bub**

- Purple pill body (#5724E9)
- White oval eyes
- Friendly, playful expression
- Can have variations (e.g., BubMoneyTongue with green tongue and dollar sign)
